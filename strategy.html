How It Works

Three Rules. Fully Systematic.

No discretion, no gut calls. The same logic runs every day.

Rule 01

Trend Filter

Only hold the asset when its price is above the 200-day moving average. When price drops below, move to cash. One filter eliminates the worst of bear markets.

Rule 02

Volatility Targeting

Scale position size inversely to 20-day realized volatility, targeting 8% annual portfolio vol. High-vol environments automatically shrink exposure — before a crisis deepens, not after.

Rule 03

No Leverage

Position is hard-capped at 100% of capital. In calm, trending markets you're fully invested. In choppy or falling markets, you're partially or fully out — protecting capital instead of chasing returns.
